Constructing the Brief.

A meeting will be held at a place of your choosing as a supplementary addition to the brief you have created, or in the interest of creating such a brief. The meeting serves as an opportunity for both parties to clear up any uncertainties and to assemble all requirements that may be needed to proceed. A decision on whether to use our services can be made either on the day or following. Construction of the brief is an entirely free service with no strings attached.

Collaboration Requirements.

If you decide on using the services offered, a chain of contact will be established to ensure you can contact the team where needed or to confirm project progress. File sharing and video collaboration will also be available through Google Drive and Skype to allow for progress reports and client input. Not only matching but exceeding your expectations is our #1 goal.

Research, Planning and Design.

If you decide to use the services offered, our experienced team will get to work immediately by extensively researching the specific needs of the proposed site as well as the functionality and aesthetic design of companies offering similar services to your own. Then, following further collaboration as desired, the planning and design stages will take place, including the gathering of all requirements, completion of aesthetic design, and any additional functionality designs. A final collaboration will be held to ensure client satisfaction or implement changes.

Product Creation and Testing.

The final product will be constructed to current standards and as swiftly as possible without sacrificing attention to detail or client needs. Rigorous testing will follow, ensuring clean compatibility across multiple devices and browsers.

Product Presentation with Hosting.

At the final stage of business, the client is presented the product, with the promise of continued, prompt support and help with hosting - when the product is web based.
